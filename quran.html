God and His Scriptures

God (Allah)

  • We believe there is no God but the One God. The Oneness of God is described in Surah Al-Ikhlas to the right and often recited in units of prayers and worth the same as 1/3 of the Quran. Allah tells us who He is by the names and attributes He uses to describe Himself within the Quran. The most common attribute He uses is that of "Ar-Rahman" translating to the Most Merciful. We learn about Allah and call on him by these names. Check out this list, this lecture series by Dr. Omar Suleiman, this book by Jinan Yousef, and this quizlet of names to learn more.

Contiuation of Revelation

Final Revelation

  • The Quran is the final revelation sent to all of humanity, preserved in its original language identical to how it was revealed 1400 years ago. The Quran provides guidance for all aspects of life and is a continuation of the previous revelations from previous prophets such as Mosus and Jesus (peace be upon all the prophets). The Quran is memorized by milions and is recited daily around the world.
